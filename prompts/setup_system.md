# RULES FOR SETUP ACTIVITIES

* Estimates are planning ranges.
* Actual replaces estimate when known once the entry is marked Complete or Unexpected.
* Unless the row is marked Complete or Unexpected, an Actual value of 0.00 means no money has been spent or received for that item yet.
* Refunds are positive inflows.
